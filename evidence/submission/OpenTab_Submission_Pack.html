01 · The product

A checkout customers understand

OpenTab turns a merchant offer into a shareable link or QR. Customers authenticate with Google or email, review a clear total, and approve without installing a browser wallet or manually choosing a payment network.

Core truth: a provider response is not payment proof. OpenTab treats a matching, confirmed, canonical Arbitrum OrderPaid event as authoritative.

One offer can be shared online or presented at a physical venue.

Familiar customer journey

No extension, seed phrase, manual bridge, network switch, or destination-gas setup in the ordinary checkout experience.

Complete merchant loop

Products, inventory, orders, settlement, refunds, withdrawals, loyalty, analytics, links, and QR sharing.

Proof without clutter

Normal users see calm commerce. A separate public-safe Judge Mode exposes the integration and settlement evidence.

02 · Merchant experience

One console from storefront to settlement

Deterministic interface capture. Values are synthetic and shown only to demonstrate product behavior.

Overview

Confirmed revenue, returning customers, recent orders, and settlement freshness at a glance.

Products

Create and manage prices, inventory, availability, purchase limits, event information, checkout links, and QR codes.

Orders and balance

Track pending and confirmed states separately. Only confirmed settlement becomes paid revenue or withdrawable balance.

Settings and operations

Manage storefront information, support details, exports, refunds, withdrawals, and guarded feature controls.

Exact

Integer-based USDC accounting—never JavaScript floating point for token base units.

Reorg-aware

The indexer verifies confirmations and canonical block hashes before updating merchant projections.

Recoverable

Saved operations and idempotency controls prevent refreshes and repeated clicks from creating duplicate payments.

03 · Customer and identity experience

Familiar access, durable results

Deterministic payment-review capture. The interface shows item cost, estimated payment cost, maximum total, and available balance before approval.

Deterministic receipt/pass capture. No live payment is represented by this screenshot.

Magic authentication

Google or email sign-in provisions the embedded EOA. OpenTab exchanges the DID token for an opaque, server-managed session.

Same-account EIP-7702 design

Particle Universal Accounts is configured with explicit useEIP7702: true; the Magic EOA remains the owner address.

After checkout

Confirmed customers receive a durable receipt/pass, loyalty progress, refund state, and privacy-safe split-reimbursement tools.
